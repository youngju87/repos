2. **Page Setup:**
   - Margins: 1" all sides
   - Clear section breaks between major sections
   - Page numbers in footer

3. **Typography:**
   - Section headers: Bold, 14pt, brand color (#0f172a)
   - Subsection headers: Bold, 12pt, accent color (#2563eb)
   - Body text: 11pt, clear and readable
   - Questions: Bold
   - Fill-in areas: Underlined or in gray text boxes

4. **Colors:**
   - Use brand colors sparingly for headers
   - Keep most content black for printing
   - Consider light gray background for fill-in boxes

5. **Spacing:**
   - Adequate space for handwritten or typed responses
   - Clear visual separation between questions
   - Group related questions together

6. **Professional Elements:**
   - Company logo/header on first page
   - Page numbers on all pages except cover
   - Professional but friendly tone
   - Clear instructions throughout

7. **Digital vs Print:**
   - For digital: Use form fields that can be filled electronically
   - For print: Ensure adequate space for handwriting
   - Consider creating both versions

8. **Export Options:**

   - Export to PDF for sending to clients
